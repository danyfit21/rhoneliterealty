## Quick reference

- **Primary color:** `#C9A24A` (muted gold) — use sparingly as a seal, not a flood
- **Background:** `#171514` (deep charcoal) or `#0B1320` (midnight blue)
- **Text:** `#F4F1EC` (soft ivory)
- **Display font:** Cormorant Garamond (serif, monumental)
- **Body font:** Inter (sans, weight 300)
- **Motion:** GSAP `power3.out` / `expo.out`, 1200–1800ms durations. No bounce, no elastic.
- **Tone:** Quiet authority. No exclamations. No emoji. Spanish as strategic accent only.
- **MMA reference:** Metaphor only — discipline, precision, resilience. Never the cage.
- **Gold rule:** If >3% of screen pixels are gold, you are oversaturated.
